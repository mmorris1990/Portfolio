Hello and welcome to my portfolio site! I am a web developer in the making, currently undergoing an intensive bootcamp course located in Nashville, TN. I have a strong background in customer service, a solid understanding of inventory management & organization, and a passion for technology. Multiple years' experience in level-one software and hardware diagnostics, troubleshooting, and repair has helped me continue my passion at a professional level, but in my free time I am always tinkering with computer and gaming hardware. I believe in taking pride in one's work and always striving for improvement, whether in self-motivated or group-oriented fashion.

I try and maintaina good life balance, and the dozens of easily accessible hiking spots around this state make it easy to be outdoors.
